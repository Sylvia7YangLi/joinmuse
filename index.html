Matching

Quality over quantity (宁缺毋滥)

Weighted preference ranking

  • You set preference weights (values, lifestyle, education, ambition, attraction, etc.).
  • Muse ranks candidates by mutual fit (two-way score, not one-way).
  • You receive 1–3 best-fit matches/day — no endless feed.

No swipe anxiety

  • No left/right swiping loop.
  • No waiting for a “like back” just to start talking.
  • Less guessing → more confidence → more real dates.

Trust

Private signals that protect good users

Private reflection (10 seconds)

  • Optional, quick check-in after a chat/date.
  • Improves personalization over time.

Anonymous experience feedback

  • Anonymous flags for rude behavior, no-shows, or unsafe conduct.
  • Repeated negative signals are down-ranked and limited.
  • No public ratings — signals are private to avoid score culture.
